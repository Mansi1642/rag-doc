=== Microsoft 2024 Annual Report – Key Highlights ===

• Revenue: Over $245 billion in annual revenue, up 16% year-over-year  
• Operating Income: Exceeded $109 billion, a 24% increase versus last year

Strategic Priorities:
1. **Security First**  
   - Launched the Secure Future Initiative (SFI), integrating cybersecurity across the organization.

2. **AI & Cloud Leadership**  
   - Continued scaling cloud services.
   - Pushed forward trustworthy AI innovation across products and services.

3. **Operational Efficiency**  
   - Implemented dynamic cost management to drive long-term operating leverage.

“As we move forward, we are focused on these three priorities to ensure continued growth and resilience.”
